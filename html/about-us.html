About Us

We are a passionate team of five students participating in our very first hackathon. Starting from scratch, we’ve come together to take on this challenge and push our skills to the next level. Our team is made up of three Web Application Development (DAW) students—David Gámiz, Trasti Gonzalez, and Alejandra Chaves—and two Mobile Application Development (DAM) students—Nazar Zemnukhov and Autman El Aabad. With a shared love for technology and problem-solving, we’re excited to collaborate, learn, and create something innovative during this event!
